★ ABOUT THE GAME

★ THE POINT

A game with a point.

On real roads, anything can happen. A gator on a Florida highway. A drifter doing donuts. A BMW that signals left and swerves right. A wide load you can't predict. A pothole at 2 a.m.

You can't memorize every hazard. The DMV manual covers the rules. The road throws in everything else. EXAM SKIPPER 3000 puts you in that traffic. Stay sharp. Respect everyone. Protect yourself.

★ THREE TENETS

  • STAY SHARP Roads change every second. Distraction costs lives. Look ahead, scan sides, expect the unexpected.
  • RESPECT EVERYONE The student driver, the slow Prius, the soccer-mom SUV, even the BMW that lies. They're all going home.
  • PROTECT YOURSELF Hold your line. Know when to brake. Don't trust strangers' turn signals. Repair what you can; survive what you can't.
